Nominating Ballot for the 2024 Hugo Awards, Lodestar Award for Best Young Adult Book, and Astounding Award for Best New Writer


Deadline

All ballots must be received by Saturday, 9 March 2024, 16:00 Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) (UTC+0)

How to Nominate

You may nominate up to 5 persons or works in each category. You are permitted (and encouraged) to make fewer nominations or none at all if you are not familiar with the candidates in a category. The order in which you list your nominations has no effect on the outcome.

  1. Enter your nominations in the text fields of the appropriate categories
  2. Click ‘Save as you go’ to save what you have currently input into the ballot
    1. ‘Save all’ at the bottom of the page performs the same function
    2. This form does not autosave!
  3. Complete your nominations, and return to make as many changes as you want up until the deadline of Saturday, 9 March 2024, 16:00 Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) (UTC+0)
  4. Send yourself an email copy of your ballot by selecting ‘Email my nominations to me’

“No Award” will appear automatically in every single category on the final ballot - there is no need to include that choice on the nomination form.

Eligibility and Rules

Category eligibility rules are included below with the specific categories.

Details of eligibility for the 2024 Hugo Awards, including works with extended eligibility and exclusions, may be found on the Glasgow 2024 web page here: https://glasgow2024.org/hugo-awards/hugo-awards-nomination/.

The full rules for the Hugo Awards are contained in Article 3 of the WSFS Constitution at http://www.wsfs.org/rules-of-the-world-science-fiction-society/.

A Chinese-language version of Article 3 of the WSFS Constitution is available here: https://glasgow2024.org/hugo-awards/hugo-awards-nomination-chinese/.

Need Help?

If you have any other questions about the process or need assistance with nominating, please contact us at hugo-help@glasgow2024.org

If you have difficulty using, reading, or understanding this form due to a disability, please contact accessibility@glasgow2024.org and hugo-help@glasgow2024.org.

2024年雨果奖、北极星奖-最佳青少年图书奖、惊奇奖-最佳新人奖提名选票


截止时间

所有选票必须在格林威治时间（GMT）（UTC+0）2024年3月9日周六16:00前由雨果奖评选委员会收讫。

如何提名

你可以在每个奖项类别中提名最多5位个人或5部作品。如果你不熟悉某个类别中的作品，你可以做出少于5个提名或不进行提名（这也是我们所提倡的）。你排列提名内容的顺序不会影响结果。

  1. 在相应奖项类别的文本框中填入你的提名。
  2. 点击“中途保存”（Save as you go）以保存你当前填入选票的内容。
    1. 页面底端的“全部保存”（Save all）可以起到同样的作用。
    2. 本表格不会自动保存！
  3. 完成提名，在格林威治时间（GMT）（UTC+0）2024年3月9日周六16:00的截止时间前可随时返回修改。
  4. 点击“将提名发送至我的邮箱”（Email my nominations to me），以电子邮件形式将你的选票发到你的邮箱。

决选投票中，每个奖项类别中都会自动出现“奖项空缺”选项——你无需将其填入提名表中。

资格与规则

各奖项类别的参评资格规则列在下面的相应奖项中。

有关2024年雨果奖参评资格的具体情况，包括具有延期资格的作品以及被排除的人员，参见2024年格拉斯哥世界科幻大会网页：https://glasgow2024.org/hugo-awards/hugo-awards-nomination-chinese/。

雨果奖的完整规则列在《世界科幻协会章程》第三条中：http://www.wsfs.org/rules-of-the-world-science-fiction-society/。
