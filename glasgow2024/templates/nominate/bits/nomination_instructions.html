Nominating Ballot for the 2024 Hugo Awards, Lodestar Award for Best Young Adult Book, and Astounding Award for Best New Writer Deadline

All ballots must be received by Saturday, 9 March 2024, 16:00 Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) (UTC+0)

How to Nominate

Enter your nominations in the text fields of the appropriate categories below. This form does not autosave. You will need to click the 'Save this Category' buttons at the bottom of each category, or 'Save All' at the bottom of the page.

When you save your ballot below, you are signing your ballot as {{ request.user.convention_profile.preferred_name }}.

You may nominate up to five persons or works in each category. However, if you are not familiar with the works that fall into that category, you are permitted (and encouraged) to make fewer nominations or none at all. Your nominations are equally weighted – the order in which you list them has no effect on the outcome.

“No Award” will appear automatically in every single category on the final ballot – there is no need to include that choice on the nomination form.

You may return to this form and make as many changes as you would like to your nomination ballot up until the deadline of Saturday, 9 March 2024, 16:00 Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) (UTC+0).

If you would like to receive an email receipt of your nomination ballot, click the 'Email my nominations to me' button at the top of this page.

Eligibility to Nominate

You may nominate for the 2024 Hugo Awards, the Lodestar Award for Best Young Adult Book, and the Astounding Award for Best New Writer, if:

• You were a WSFS member of the 2023 Chengdu Worldcon • You are a WSFS member of the Glasgow 2024 Worldcon on or before 31 January 2024, 23:59 Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) (UTC+0)

Only members of Glasgow 2024 (including those who join after 31 January 2024) will be eligible to vote on the final ballot.

Only natural persons may nominate, and each natural person may cast only one nomination ballot for the 2024 Hugo Awards.

If you have any other questions about the process or need assistance with nominating, please contact us at hugo-help@glasgow2024.org
